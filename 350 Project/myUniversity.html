    • When was BMCC founded?

    Borough of Manhattan Community college was founded in 1963. It originally opened in 1964 as a small, primarily business-oriented community college offering programs aimed at the business community.

    BMCC is a vibrant community college located in New York City. This college offer a wide range of programs and opportunities for students.

    Strengths of BMCC

  • BMCC's tuition cost is significantly lower than a four-year college. BMCC's tuition cost will save students thousands of dollars on their early education.

  • BMCC is a top #3 community college nationally, recognized for supporting minority students, academic research and many more factors.

  • There are many flexible course options for students. Classes can be hybrid, asynchronous, on the weekends, etc.
